Case: A complete redesign of a mature product with huge auditory

Role: Concept developing, prototyping, design supervision

When redesign started, the Kompas 3D had 23 years of history, 16 versions, and a huge audience as in Russia as around a word. The current market challenges required a new, modern product. The management of the Company had decided to renew the Kompas 3D interface and improve the usability of it. All that jazz led to total product redesign.

Initial requirements:

  • Make the modern interface with a long lifecycle
  • Provide all screens resolution compatibility
  • Improve product usability and accessibility
  • Provide all Kompas 3D power to third party developers

After the product study and competitors analysis, I developed the new concept of the interface.

The concept included a total redesign of all of the entire product interfaces in a new unified, recognizable as Kompas, timeless style. All base user scenarios were gently reworked, but most interactions stayed familiar to users.

Fundamental business requirements decomposed to the next functional blocs:

  1. Main window, panels and toolbars
  2. Messageboxes
  3. Dialogs
  4. Special Dialogs (Tollerance, Color etc.)
  5. Processes (Inside the comand)
  6. Trees ()
  7. Installer
  8. Splash Screen
  9. Icon Fonts
  10. Themes and Colors
  11. Style Guides
  12. Suplemental apps for testing needs
